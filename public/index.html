Info

Lifeathon- Season 2 is being launched by Christ College of Engineering , Irinjalakuda with KSUM as associating partner.Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) plays major role in promoting the technical ideas of students.IEDC of Christ College of Engineering helps the innovators to achieve their dreams by giving them proper mentoring and all kinds of support.Events conducted by IEDC CCE has enabled students to flourish in their innovative fields.The event LIFEATHON-Season 2 aims to Connect Students with Startups thereby giving students an opportunity to solve the pressing problem statements given by Startups.

Timeline
  • Release of problem statements and starting of online submission of solution: 15/09/2021
  • Last date for solution submission has been extended: 17/10/2021
  • Declaration of shortlisted solutions: 18/10/2021
  • Presentation of solutions: 20/10/2021
  • Prototype progress review: 25/11/2021-30/11/2021
  • Submission of prototype: 10/12/2021
Submission Guidelines
  • Students from any AICTE approved institution(all over India)can participate.
  • Participants can form a team consisting of 3-5 members in which a girl member is compulsory.
  • Teams can have inter-disciplinary participants.
  • Only new and original ideas will be considered for the contest.
  • Innovative solutions in their prescribed format should be sent to the following lifeathon@cce.edu.in
  • One team can submit solutions for more than one problem statement.More than one solution for the same problem statement from a team is not invited.
WHAT IS LIFEATHON?

Connecting Students with Start-ups

LIFEATHON is an innovative hackathon event organized at Christ College of Engineering Irinjalakuda. This platform serves as a fruitful event for the students to interact and learn with experienced mentors, professional companies and an opportunity to focus on Skilling and Upskilling. Lifeathon will also create a pathway for students to get internships and placements in startups.

Throw Back...

An Overview of LIFEATHON Season 01

The first edition of lifeathon named "LIFEATHON – a Socio-Technical Hackathon” was organized as a bridging between academia and societal problems.The Advisory panel consisted of Doctorate holders, Class C Scientists, Chief Officers of various renowned companies, from all over India. From 16th January 2019 to 25th of January, 2019, the team of lifeathon collected almost 25 problems from different walks of life. There were over 20 team registrations. The best solution for each of the problem statements were funded through this event. Each team was continuously monitored and mentored for a period of 3 months within which they developed the prototype. It was then handed over to the respective beneficiaries during the valedictory function of LIFEATHON which was held on 10th May 2019.
