A mobile app to encourage small-scale urban farms..

Organistaion:Eyewey

Domain :AI, Networking & Supply Chains


"Urban farms are on the rise with specific trend in rise of roof-top farming and backyard organic farms. A mobile app is required for both Android/IOS/Flutter with seamless UI/Ux workflow to provide easy access for urban farmers to information on homegrown crops, common disease detection on these crops through the use of computer vision along with prescription of remedies and allow them to connect with their nearest agriculture society for support, updates, sale and distribution. Through the platform of 'Eyewey', the participating teams will be provided with datasets, trained AI-models for detection. They will also be provided with the base mobile templates in Android and IOS for the seamless integration of the AI models for detection. If the participating team wishes to expand the scope of the datasets, the right support for training the AI Models for detection will be provided. The participating teams are required to focus on creating a seamless workflow to house these features since the target market audience are expected to be between 35-55 years and should be assumed to have limited tolerance to adopting new technologies. The most elegant and simplest UI/Ux workflows will be preferred. "
